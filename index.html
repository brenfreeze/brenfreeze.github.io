01.About Me

Hello! I'm Bren, a software engineer based in Las Piñas, Philippines. My journey into web development began with a deep curiosity for how things work, which led me to build my first web apps and eventually pursue it as a career. I thrive on turning ideas into reality through code, creating interfaces that are not only functional but also enjoyable to use.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working on a diverse range of projects, from augmented reality solutions and e-commerce platforms to real estate applications and brand management systems. I've also had the opportunity to lead teams and mentor fellow developers, an experience that has been incredibly rewarding.
